# MITRE ATT&CK Framework

A free, globally accessible knowledge base of adversary
tactics and techniques based on real-world observations. Organized as tactics
(columns) and techniques (rows). Book 1 uses it as its chapter spine.

## Tactics covered here
The chapters implemented in [[book-python-for-cybersecurity]] map to:
[[initial-access]], [[persistence]], [[credential-access]], [[exfiltration]]
(and more).
