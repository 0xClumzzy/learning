# Scapy

The packet manipulation library underpinning most of
Understanding Network Hacks. Build/parse/send packets at any layer, read/write
PCAP, and craft custom protocol frames.

Foundational for [[arp-spoofing]], [[port-scanning]], [[dns-spoofing]],
[[wifi-attacks]]. Lower-level sibling: [[python-sockets]].
From [[book-understanding-network-hacks]].
